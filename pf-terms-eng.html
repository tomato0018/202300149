Perfume Terms

Term Name: Volume More Details
URI https://tomato0018.github.io/202300149.github.io/volume.html
Definition Refers to the total volume of perfume contained within the bottle, which may also be expressed in weight in some cases.
Comment Capacity is described using appropriate units such as millimeters (ml), fl.oz, gram (g), etc.

Perfume Terms

Term Name: Container Material (용기 재질) More Details
URI https://tomato0018.github.io/202300149.github.io/containermaterial.html
Definition Refers to the type of material used to create the container that holds the perfume.
Comment 유리(Glass), 플라스틱(Plastic), 세라믹(Ceramic), 도자기(porcelain) , 금속(Metal), 스테인리스 스틸(Stainless Steel), 재활용재료(Recycled Materials)등으로 기록할 수 있다.
Term Name: Container Shape More Details
URI https://tomato0018.github.io/202300149.github.io/containershape.html
Definition Refers to the physical structure and design of the container that holds the perfume.
Comment It can be expressed in a variety of artistic and creative designs, including cylindrical, oval, circular, gourd, and square shapes.
Term Name: Cap Material More Details
URI https://tomato0018.github.io/202300149.github.io/capmaterial.html
Definition Refers to the type of material used to create the cap that covers the opening of the perfume bottle.
Comment There are glass, plastic, ceramic, porcelain, metal, stainless steel, and recycled materials.
Term Name: Cap Shape More Details
URI https://tomato0018.github.io/202300149.github.io/capshape.html
Definition Refers to the physical structure and design of the cap that covers the opening of the perfume bottle.
Comment It can be expressed in a variety of artistic and creative designs, including cylindrical, oval, circular, and square shapes.
Term Name: Type More Details
URI https://tomato0018.github.io/202300149.github.io/type.html
Definition Refers to the classification of perfume based on its physical state or application method.
Comment It comes in the form of solid, liquid, gel, oil, mist/aerosol, and powder.
Term Name: Dispensing Type More Details
URI https://tomato0018.github.io/202300149.github.io/dispensingtype.html
Definition Refers to the method of delivering perfume from the container to the skin or another surface.
Comment Liquids are discharged in the form of spray, roll-on, pump, drop, or mist, and solids can be applied directly to the skin.
Term Name: Frangrance composition More Details
URI https://tomato0018.github.io/202300149.github.io/fragrancecomposition.html
Definition Refers to the combination of various scents experienced in the perfume as it develops.
Comment Depending on the stage of scent development, it is divided into top notes such as citrus, bergamot, and lavender, middle notes such as floral, spicy, and fruity, and base notes such as vanilla, musk, and sandalwood.
Term Name: Longevity More Details
URI https://tomato0018.github.io/202300149.github.io/longevity.html
Definition Refers to how long the fragrance of the perfume lasts on the skin or clothing.
Comment The duration of time is described with Arabic numerals and an appropriate time unit.
Term Name: Ingredients More Details
URI https://tomato0018.github.io/202300149.github.io/Ingredients.html
Definition Refers to the substances used to create and compose the perfume.
Comment There are essential oils, fragrance compounds, alcohol, distilled water, and fixatives.
